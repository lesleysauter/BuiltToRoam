Hiking Alone?

Or, you're completely new to exploring trails?

Here's some information that can be helpful for you to know before going on your first adventure either alone or for the first time all together. Checklist of supplies are highly recommended!

Checklist of supplies

  • Water, as much of it as you comfortably bring with you!
  • Snacks
  • Change of socks (in case you fully submerge your feet in water)
  • First aid kit
  • Bear repellent (if in a bear populated area)
  • Download a map of the trail on your phone or bring a paper map if possible!
  • Sunscreen
  • Misquito and tick repellent
